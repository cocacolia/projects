Hi! I'm Olia Hordiienko, a Ukrainian graphic designer, illustrator, and art teacher originally from Kyiv, now residing in Karlsruhe, Germany. I'm passionate about design because it serves as a visual language, enabling meaningful communication. Above all, I cherish human connections, and I believe design plays a crucial role in fostering them. In my work, I emphasize playfulness, interactivity, and accessibility of information. I specialize in layout design, visual identities, gamified projects, and illustration.

I studied in Kyiv Polytechnic Institute, then in Academy of Fine arts in Kyiv and finally in High School of Design in Karlsruhe. I am engaging as an educator sometimes too. Feel free to reach out if you're in need of a creative collaborator for your project!
